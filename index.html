I study how we are able to make skillful hand movements. In my research, I use functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) and neural recordings to study how neurons in the cerebral cortex function together to produce skilled hand movements.

Currently, I am a senior Neuroscience PhD candidate co-supervised by Dr. Jörn Diedrichsen and Dr. Andrew Pruszynski at The Brain and Mind Institute, University of Western Ontario, London (ON), Canada.
