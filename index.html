I study the sensorimotor control of hands. More specifically, I am interested in how processes in our brains allow us to control our hands with relative ease. To study these processes, I use functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) and neural recordings.

Currently, I am pursuing a PhD in Neuroscience with Dr. Jörn Diedrichsen and Dr. Andrew Pruszynski at The Brain and Mind Institute, University of Western Ontario (Canada).

I am funded by NSERC and some of my projects have funding from BrainsCAN.
