Spencer A. Arbuckle

I am interested in how primates are able to perform highly complex hand movements. Skillful hand use reflects intelligent integration of tactile sensory information with motor plans. In my PhD work, I have focused on the role of the primary sensorimotor cortices in hand control: both from the perspectives of motor control and tactile sensory integration.
