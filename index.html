I use data to help teams tackle complex healthcare problems.

I’ve had the privilege of collaborating with great people across clinical research, health technology, and public instutions, including:

  • PurposeMed, advancing equitable access to care;
  • Phagenesis, restoring swallowing function after stroke;
  • MindMaze, scaling neurorehabilitation through digital therapeutics;
  • Statistics Canada, using alternative (non-survey data, such as geospatial and transactional data) data to track health metrics;
  • Canadian Science Policy Centre, supporting science and innovation policy;
  • Queen's University, launching a neurotechnology learning platform;
  • Western University, advancing our understanding of the neural control of the hand.
